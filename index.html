Welcome to South West Equestrian

We are a family run riding school residing along the beautiful coast of Donegal. We offer a range of services from beginners lessons to beach rides and everything in between. With an indoor and outdoor arena we can cater for all your needs all year round. Make a booking or get in touch today through our contact form.

Lessons

We offer a range of classes from beginner to advanced which are taught by friendly, experienced instructors with the highest qualifications. Classes are offered on a daily basis with plenty of variety to suit your needs.

Events

We host a range of events for all levels including show-jumping, dressage and cross-country which are open to all riders to compete in. Competitions are hosted all year round with prizes sponsored by local businesses.

Summer Camps

In the summer months we run week long camps for kids of all ages where we teach the fundamentals of caring for your horse as well as fun games and lessons all day long. The camps run for one week at a time with discounts offered for families as well as bulk bookings.

Therapy Horse Riding

Here at South West Equestrian we offer therapeutic riding lessons with some of our specially trained horses. Theraphy horse riding has been used to promote relaxation, improve physical and emotional skills as well as being a healthy and fun activity.
