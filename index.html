Welcome to South West Equestrian

We are a family run riding school residing along the beautiful coast of Donegal. We offer a range of services from beginners lessong to beach rides and everything in between. With an indoor and outdoor arena we can cater for all your needs all year round. Get in touch with our enquiries form below.

Lessons

We offer a range of classes from beginner to advanced and everything in between taught by friendly, experienced instructors with the highest qualifications. Classes are offered on a daily basis with plenty of variety to suit your needs.

Events

We host a range of events for all levels including show-jumping, dressage and cross-country which are open to all riders to compete in. Competitions are hosted all year round with prizes sponsored by local businesses.

Summer Camps

In the summer months we run week long camps for kids of all ages where we teach the fundamentals of caring for your horse as well as fun games and lessons all day long. The camps run for one week at a time with discounts for two weeks or more and families.

Therapy Horses

South West Equestrian is home to a few beautifully gentle therapy horses which we use for our therapeutic riding lessons. Theraphy horse riding has been used to promote relaxation, improve physical and emotional skills as well as being a healthy and fun activity.
